Explore Our Herbs

Discover the rich heritage of medicinal plants used in AYUSH traditions. Click "Know More" to view details about each plant.

Aloe Vera (Aloe vera)

Known as the "Plant of Immortality," prized for its soothing, healing, and skin-rejuvenating properties.

Beech Tree (Fagus sp.)

Known as the "Mother of the Forest," valued for its dense canopy, smooth bark, and role in woodland ecosystems.

Herb Robert (Geranium robertianum)

Known as "Nature's Antibiotic," cherished for its antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and immune-boosting properties.

Wild lettuce (Lactuca virosa)

Known as "Opium Lettuce," valued for its natural sedative and pain-relieving properties.

Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)

Known as the "Calming Herb," treasured for its soothing aroma, stress-relief, and sleep-enhancing properties.

Dandelion (Taraxacum officinale)

Known as the "Detox Herb," celebrated for its liver-cleansing, digestive, and diuretic benefits.
